## Full Boilerplate

PrismaX is a robotics-native service layer that sets the standards for how robots, data, and human work are deployed to advance intelligence for physical AI systems. By operating human-in-the-loop systems in real environments, PrismaX defines how robotic work generates data and how that data is translated into intelligent behavior. These standards serve as a shared deployment protocol, allowing robotics and AI companies to deploy systems correctly, learn faster, and progress toward autonomy with confidence.

Founded by a team with deep experience across robotics, AI, and crypto, PrismaX is building durable infrastructure that helps people and robots work together in the real world. PrismaX is part of the NVIDIA Inception Program and is backed by an $11M seed round led by a16z crypto CSX.
