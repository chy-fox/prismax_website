# Core Pillars

The three pillars that describe what PrismaX builds. Each pillar pairs an **Asset** (what PrismaX provides) with a **System** (the operational layer that produces or uses it).

Pull definitions verbatim — do not paraphrase. Canonical source for the marketing system: `../../Prismax-marketing/brand/product-pillars.md`.

---

## Robots

PrismaX defines the embodiments physical AI should take for specific goals and environments, ensuring capability matches context from the start.

- **Asset:** Robots
- **System:** Deployment

---

## Data

PrismaX specifies the characteristics of high-quality robotics data, including how it should be captured and what makes it useful for learning.

- **Asset:** Data
- **System:** Data Generation

---

## Intelligence

PrismaX connects human-in-the-loop operation and structured data generation directly to model training, allowing intelligence to improve across tasks, environments, and robots.

- **Asset:** Intelligence
- **System:** Learning
