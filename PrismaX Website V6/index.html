and VLA models.

VLA Foundry captures real robotic work from vetted owner-operators, QA'd by the community against the Operator Intent standard — the raw material for training physical AI.

Explore VLA Foundry →
01 / Real demandSourced

Tasks backed by real demand.

Every task in VLA Foundry is sourced from real robotics demand — not synthetic prompts. Flowers arranged, medicine sorted, pipes tightened, clothes folded.

02 / OperatorsReal HW

Owner-operators upload real data.

Vetted robotics manufacturers run real sessions on real hardware and upload the resulting interaction data. Real operation — not labeling.

03 / Operator IntentCommunity QA

QA against a shared standard.

Community reviewers score submissions against the Operator Intent standard — smoothness, diversity, and format — and earn for valid contributions.

04 / Standards loopFeedback

A standards feedback loop.

PrismaX trains internal research models on VLA Foundry output. Lessons from that training feed back into the QA criteria.
