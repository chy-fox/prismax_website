- **Cards:**
  - `01 · REAL DEMAND` — **Tasks backed by real demand.** — Every task comes from real robotics demand, not synthetic prompts. Arranging flowers, sorting medicine, tightening pipes, folding clothes.
  - `02 · OWNER-OPERATORS` — **Owner-operators upload real data.** — Vetted manufacturers run real sessions on real hardware and upload the interaction data. Real operation, not labeling.

  - `04 · STANDARDS LOOP` — **A standards feedback loop.** — PrismaX trains research models on what comes out of the Foundry. The lessons feed back into the QA criteria. Better data each cycle.
